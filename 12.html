12: Designing, Defining, and Triggering CSS3 Transforms

CSS3 transforms allow you to translate, rotate, scale, and skew elements.
A transformation is an effect that lets an element change shape, size and position.
CSS3 supports 2D and 3D transformations.


Very Simple Transform

When your cursor hovers over the red square rotates the square by 25 degrees. The square goes back when you cursor stops hovering.




Less Simple Transform

The sliders below control the x, y, and z axis rotation. I was
just going to use a simple div to show how they work but then I
wondered if I could 3D rotate the canvas I made a few pages back.
Whaddaya know? It worked.
I'd like to have the settings hold so the rotation could be on
several planes at once, but that'll have to wait till later.

Rotate X:

transform: rotate x(7deg);
Rotate Y:

transform: rotate y(7deg);
Rotate Z:

transform: rotate z(7deg);
